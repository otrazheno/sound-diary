“Self-portrait as a Meditation on Somewhere Else” is the second entry in self-portrait series. I made it my goal while Artist in Residence of the Everglades to hike every trail available, to journey deep into the waters, to explore the perim- eter, and capture a complete audio sketchbook of the park and my time there. From human noise pollution to the rarely heard sounds of wildlife underwater, this piece is a non-linear collage and meditation of this place and our relationship to it.

(Self-portrait as a meditation on Somewhere Else by Koree Reeder)
